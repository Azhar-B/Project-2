The Cast of Characters

  • Smedley Butler: Marine General and Empire's greatest soldier turned radical.
  • The Business Bureau: A collection of the country's richest men who seek to overthrow any goverment that would limit their advancement and wealth.
  • The American Legion: an organization built support veterans and suppress labor.

Highlights of the Event

  • The Bonus Army, a group of veterans march on Washington, wanting payments for their bonds due to the Great Depression.
  • FDR is elected. He begins instituting his New Deal Program. This worries a collection of the wealthiest magnates and capitalists in America
  • They attempt to get Smedley Butler, an American Marine General and Empire's greatest solider to help organize the American Legion
  • Butler quickly learns that the American Legion is a front to be used a private army for the Collective to force the goverment to support their policy plans.
  • He gets an investigative journalist friend who records the meetings with the Collective's representatives.
  • This is brought to Congress. An investigation is had, but none of the conspirators are brought to justice.
