The Business Plot

The Business Plot was an attempt by the wealthiest businessmen of the time to overthrow President Franklin D. Roosevelt and establish a fascist dictatorship.

The Cast of Characters

Smedley Butler: Marine General and Empire's greatest soldier turned radical The Business Bureau: A collection of the country's richest men who seek to overthrow any goverment that would limit their advancement and wealth. The American Legion: an organization built support veterans and suppress labor.
